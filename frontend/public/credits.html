Credits

UNI! is made by a small crew who wanted to build a card game they'd actually want to play themselves. Here's who did what, and a thank-you to everyone whose work we're borrowing along the way.

Made by

Eldyn — Creator and lead developer. Built the game engine, the multiplayer backend, and most of the pixel art, and is the first person to blame for any bug you just ran into.

Fede — Art and concept. Shaped a lot of UNI!'s visual identity and creative direction.

Bluu — Development and concept support. Helped push features and ideas across the finish line.

Music

Birdrun — Music. Check out more of their work over on Bandcamp.

Attributions

Thank you for creating. UNI! stands upon your shoulders.

Our display font — the big, thick pixel typeface used for the "UNI!" logo and headings — is one we couldn't track down an original creator for, despite trying. We cleaned it up and adapted it for the web ourselves. If you made it, or know who did, we'd love to credit you properly: reach out at inquiries@playuni.app.

Some of the smaller pixel fonts in the interface (including Habbo and TinyUnicode) come from the NB Pixel Font Bundle by the Nimble Beasts Collective. Thank you to the creators.

Interface icons are from the Pixel Icon Library by HackerNoon, licensed under CC BY 4.0.

The word list behind chat's on-screen profanity masking comes from the LDNOOBW list, licensed under CC BY 4.0.

Everything else

UNI!'s source code and original assets are released into the public domain (CC0) — see the About page for more on that.

Made with love by Eldyn <3
